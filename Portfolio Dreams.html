What Are Your Goals?



No matter what your age, you will always have goals you strive to achieve and dreams you wish to come true. Now let me ask you, what are yours? What are your goals and dreams? Now think for a second. Have you really strived to achieve these, have you attempted to reach that benchmark? If you have then well done, you will surely succeed. But if you haven't, that's fine. Just rethink two things. One: why aren't you trying, what's stopping you from getting there? Or Two: Is this really what you want to become?
